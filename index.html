About

The Company

Purple Hedgehog AI LLC was founded by Dean Brown, a Senior Staff Consultant with over 20 years of enterprise technology experience specializing in ServiceNow implementations, CMDB governance, and IT asset management.

After watching organizations struggle to adopt AI responsibly—moving fast but breaking things, or moving safely but never shipping—Dean started building tools that refuse to compromise.

The result is a validation-first approach: AI that treats its own output as fallible by default. We build practical tooling for developers and enterprises who need speed without sacrificing correctness.
